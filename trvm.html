WELCOME TO THIRUVANANDAPURAM


Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, blushes in regal splendour. Such is the historic legacy that the moment you enter this district, you are transported to the age of the kings and the conquerors. With ancient palaces, royal complexes, museums and forts, Thiruvananthapuram bathes in the glory of the yesteryears. This city has everything a traveller needs. You can either walk along the royal corridors and experience the royal charm or tread through the rich natural bounties of nature. Endowed with a wide variety of flora and fauna, wildlife, beaches, mountains and backwaters, one would never be short of any activity to indulge in while being here. To be in the royal city is to be in a space and time that serenades one into a state of bliss and contentment. Life flows at a surreal pace here. Best places to visit


Kovalam beach

Several decades ago, back in the 70s, a serene beach in Thiruvananthapuram shot to fame. It became a stopover in the hippie trail from Goa to Sri Lanka and has never been the same. Over the years the beach became more of an emotion and firmly placed itself on the global tourism map. With its palm-fronted beaches, shimmering sands and a blend of unique cultural and adventurous experiences, it is one of the much-loved beaches in Kerala. This is Kovalam. Life begins with a surreal vibrance at Kovalam. Whether you choose to laze away with your favourite drink and stare into the horizon amid fishing activities, to scuttle through the alleyways experiencing the myriad cultures or get adventurous by indulging in some sporty activities.
